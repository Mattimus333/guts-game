Rules of Guts

  • Each player shall ante the specified amount into the pot

  • Each player is dealt two cards

  • After all players have decided, the players that are in will reveal their cards

  • If no player is in, all players must ante again

  • If one player is in, that player must play the pot. If they lose they must match the pot

  • If multiple players are in, the winner takes the pot and the losing players must match the pot

  • If, after a round, you don't have enough chips to match the pot, YOU LOSE!

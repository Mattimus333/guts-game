Rules of Guts

  • Each round begins with each player anteing ten chips

  • Players are dealed ten cards each and then decide to stay or hold

  • Players that stay reveal their cards and the higest two card poker hand wins

  • Pairs beat high card and suit order is used to break ties

  • If no player holds, all players must re ante

  • If only one player holds, the pot becomes the second player

  • Winner receives chips equal to the amount in the pot from each loser

  • If the pot is beaten, you can choose to re-ante and start new round

  • If, after a round, you run out of chips, YOU LOSE!
